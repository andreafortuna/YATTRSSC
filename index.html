YATTRSSC - Yet Another Tiny Tiny RSS Client

Simple client for accessing instances of Tiny Tiny RSS (http://tt-rss.org/). Allows you to read and manage your feeds, add tags and sharing posts on Facebook and Twitter.

Technology

Phonegap + JQuery Mobile 1.3

Platforms

Android (Play Store)

iOs (under Apple review)

Blackberry (under RIM review)
