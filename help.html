First configuration

When you first start you must configure the access data to your instance of TinyTinyRSS. Enter username, password and url of the instance (full url with 'http://').

Subscribe Feed

Enter the address of the site or feed, select the category and press 'Subscribe'

Navigating the post and use gestures

In the post, you can use the following gesture: Swipe to the right to assign a star Swipe left to mark the item as read In detail the post, you can swipe to the right to view the article above, left to the next
